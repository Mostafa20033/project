let's know about Tunisia

The state of Tunisia is an Arab Islamic country, green Tunisia, a country of ancient history and ancient civilization that dates back to ancient times and left behind a wonderful civilization and amazing views for tourists between ancient castles and historical buildings and various visit attractions between civilizations that passed here to leave you an opportunity to see unique tourist attractions and enjoy a wonderful time.


City the popular places of this city
Tunis One of the most famous tourist places in the capital, Tunis: Al-Zaytouna Mosque, the old city
Sousse One of the most important tourist places in Sousse is the Sousse Archaeological Museum
Carthage Magon district, an integrated neighborhood dating back to the fifth century BC
Sidi Bou Said One of the most famous tourist places in Tunisia: The Ennejma Ezzahra Palace, which has become a museum of musical instruments
Sfax One of the most important tourist places in Tunisia is Sfax, the old city, which is famous for its ancient buildings



The Tunisian capital is one of the most unpretentious cities in North Africa, yet still full of a western touch. It is this recall style that makes Tunisia an ideal backdrop for the region. Most of the city's main attractions are located in the Old Town, which is a tourist attraction in itself. Here, the forked alleys amid the tall walls are where you are bound to get lost.


Sousse It is called the pearl of the coast, and many peoples have settled in it in the past, the most important of which are the Arabs, the Byzantines, and the Romans. According to the UNESCO World Heritage site, there are a group of monuments and museums that distinguish Sousse.


Carthage It is a historic city dating back to the Carthage civilization, located northeast of the capital, Tunis, where a group of distinctive archaeological sites are spread, which has been included in the UNESCO World Heritage List for its great historical importance; Therefore, it is an important tourist destination for those interested in ancient history in the North African region.


Sidi Bou Said, one of the most important and beautiful cities in Tunisia, enjoys a charming landscape, and is also full of white-painted buildings with iron decorations and blue wooden doors, which makes it wonderful and a place to rest, and also includes historical places that attract tourists.


Sfax is one of the main commercial centers of Tunisia and the second largest city in the country. Although Sfax does not have as many sandy attractions as other Tunisian towns, it is a good place for sightseeing and a great place to enjoy Tunisian culture.
