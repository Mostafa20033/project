some of the most important tourist attractions in palestine:

1)Al-Aqsa Mosque and the Dome of the Rock

Al-Aqsa Mosque is located in the Old City of Jerusalem, and according to what was found in history, it was built 40 years after the construction of the Kaaba, and was named by this name because of its distance from the Grand Mosque. Al-Aqsa Mosque and the honorable dome are considered among the most sacred religious places. Al-Aqsa Mosque was mentioned in the Holy Qurâ€™an, specifically in Surat Al-Isra.

2)Ibrahimi Mosque

This mosque is located in the city of Hebron. It was called by that name because it houses the tomb of our master Ibrahim, peace be upon him. The Ibrahimi Mosque is surrounded by an old wall that was built about 2000 years ago.

3)Hisham's Palace

Hisham's Palace is located in the city of Jericho, and was named by that name because the Umayyad Caliph Hisham Abd al-Malik was the one who built it. In general, the meanings of innovative engineering are evident in every part of this palace; Being decorated with mosaics and striking decorations, it also includes mosques and halls full of antique columns.

4)Baha'i Gardens and the Shrine of the Bab

The Bahai Gardens are located in Haifa, one of the most beautiful gardens in the world, and the tomb of Ali Muhammad Reza Shirazi is also located in the same city.

5)Alqiama charch

This church is located in the city of Jerusalem, and it is one of the most important churches in the world; It is believed that it contains the tomb of Jesus, peace be upon him, and that he was crucified on top of it

6)Khan Al-Umdan

Ahmed Pasha al-Jazzar built Khan al-Umdan in Acre, at the end of the 18th century AD. In the past, merchants used to put goods on the first floor of the khan, and sleep on the second floor.

7)Hassan Bek Mosque

The mosque was named by this name in relation to the Ottoman ruler who ruled Jaffa during the Ottoman rule. The Hasan Bey Mosque is located near the sea in the city of Jaffa.and It contains a dome and one minaret
